City Select 2 / City Select Lux (adapters sold separately)

Here are the infant car seats you can use with the Citi Select/City Select Lux strollers. In order for the car seat to safely click into the stroller, you’ll need an adapter, which can be found in the adapter link next to each infant car seat brand.

  • Baby Jogger (adapter): City GO, City GO 2, City GO Air
  • Britax (adapter): B-Safe Gen 2, B-Safe 35, B-Safe 35 Elite, BOB B-Safe
  • Chicco (adapter): KeyFit 30
  • Graco (adapter): Snugride Click Connect 30, Snugride Click Connect 35, Snugride Click Connect 35 LX, Snugride Click Connect 40
  • Peg Perego (adapter): Primo Viaggio 4-35, Primo Viaggio 4-35 Nido
